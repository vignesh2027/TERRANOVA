How It Works
Guide · Features · Shortcuts
🌍 The Five Worlds
🌍
Earth View
Full-screen interactive globe with custom dark cartographic styling. Click anywhere to reveal coordinates, local time, and live satellite imagery of that exact region. Switch between map, satellite, terrain, and hybrid views. Measure distances between any two points on Earth.
🔭
Space Intelligence
Today's Astronomy Picture of the Day with full explanation. Watch the full disc of Earth from deep space — 1.5 million kilometres away — with images cycling every 5 seconds. Track every asteroid passing near Earth today on an animated radar display.
🌋
Natural Events Monitor
Every active wildfire, storm, earthquake, volcano, and flood on Earth — plotted live on the map. Choose which categories to display using the filter chips. Click any event to fly the camera to that location instantly.
🚀
Mission Center
Track the International Space Station in real time — position updates every 5 seconds. Browse the latest photos taken on the surface of Mars by the Curiosity rover. Follow the countdown to the next rocket launches from spaceports worldwide.

💾 Data & Privacy
All data is fetched directly from public monitoring services and cached locally in your browser. No personal data is collected. The cache expires automatically — typically within 5–30 minutes — ensuring you always see fresh intelligence. Your API settings are stored only in your own browser's local storage and never transmitted anywhere.
